## Prince / the Cat — true identity

Prince is actually Princess Aya Stronghome but she has been ensorcelled into the tomcat and cannot reveal her real person on penalty of being permanently locked in as the cat.

**Body vs. spirit (author, 2026-06-25):** the physical form is a genuine **tomcat (male body)**; the trapped, ensorcelled being inside is **female** (Princess Aya). This is *why* masculine surface address — "Prince," "old boy," "he," a tomcat — is **safe misdirection**, while any *feminine* address risks exposing the spirit and locking the curse. Narration stays strictly neutral ("it"); in-world characters read the body and assume male.

**Concealed metadata** (kept out of `03_Characters.md` per Hard Rule 1): her true Groups are **Fairy Princess** and **Ensorcelled**. In `03` her Groups line shows only "Magical Animals." Do not let "Princess," "Fairy," "Aya," "Stronghome," or "Ensorcelled" appear in `03`, prose, summaries, or AI continuations.

**Permitted (Hard Rule 1 amended 2026-06-25):** a *gender-neutral* royal honorific — *"Your Highness"* or *"Your Majesty"* — spoken sarcastically to the cat by Stepan (or anyone unaware of her identity) is allowed; it does not reveal her as a princess or female, and it is how Stepan reaches the name *"Prince."* The forbidden terms above are unchanged, and narration must never call her "she"/"Princess."

## Danica Lapczuk — a different bloodline (clerics)

Canon (author, 2026-06-26); author-only until the reveal. Danica is **not** of Stepan's lineage. She carries a **separate bloodline — one that generates clerics** (divine casters), distinct from Stepan's Shop-guardian / arcane line.

- **The Shop does not serve or restore her.** Its hospitality, SP, and Restoration are attuned to **Stepan's lineage alone** (consistent with `08` §7 bloodline-gating). She can be *in* the Shop and helping, but it gives her nothing.
- **Stats pre-set for the reveal:** Strength **17**, Wisdom **17** (raised from 15/14), with Constitution 16 and Charisma 16 — a formidable cleric profile — already entered in `03` so nothing has to change at the reveal. `03` itself says only "there is much more to Danica," with no spoiler.
- `[Inference]` Her guardedness and folklore-keeping read, in hindsight, as someone who already knows what the Shop is — because her own heritage gives her a frame for it.
- **Reveal timing:** surfaces **just before the end of Chapter 10**, as the Book 1 → Book 2 pivot. Keep it out of prose, summaries, and the web index until then.
- **Reconciles** the loose "second bloodline" note in `08` §2: Danica is a *different* lineage, **not** a co-earner on Stepan's Shop.

## The Shop's true purpose — order, chaos, and balance

Canon (author, 2026-06-25). Arthur's Curios is **ancient but decayed through neglect.** Its real function is cosmological: it holds **a place in the multiverse, defending order by *encapsulating* chaos rather than eliminating it.** It is a **containment vessel**, not a weapon — chaos is *held*, not destroyed. **The governing principle is balance.**

Implications to keep consistent:

- The curio "collection" is partly a **containment array**: some of the "junk" is chaotic or dangerous material the Shop keeps bound so the wider order holds. A guardian's true role is maintaining that containment — stewardship *is* cosmic load-bearing work, not mere tidying.
- The Shop's restraint (it withholds comfort on principle; it "does not eliminate," it balances) is the temperamental echo of this purpose. Power used to *erase* a threat runs against what the Shop is for; power used to *contain and rebalance* is aligned with it.
- **Stepan does not know any of this early** (Hard Rule 2). He experiences a decayed shop full of odd stock and a system that rewards care. The cosmic framing — and that he is its guardian in this larger sense — arrives late.

**Reveal timing:** `[Unverified]` not yet placed on the arc. Candidate: it surfaces around or after the ledger-discovery beat and the first non-human customer, once Stepan has accepted that magic is real. Cross-ref: [`06_Sentient_Objects.md` → Purpose and cosmology](06_Sentient_Objects.md).
